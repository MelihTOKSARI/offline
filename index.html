How it works

Find your thing

A curated library of real-world hobbies — crafts, outdoors, cooking, music, mini-adventures — filtered by the time you have, indoors or out, solo or social.

Log it. Your trail grows.

Every offline session becomes a stone on your illustrated journey, with photos and notes you'll want to revisit. Miss a day? The trail waits. No broken chains, no shame.

Streaks without the stress

Grow your streak by doing things, not avoiding your phone. Optional Screen Time assist can auto-confirm sessions — your usage data never leaves your device.

Better together

Add friends free, see their streaks, send one gentle nudge a day. Share your “12 hours offline this week” card when you're proud of it.

Free to use — library, logging, streaks, journey, challenges, and friends. Offline Premium ($3.99/month or $24.99/year with a 7-day free trial on annual) adds personalized recommendations, advanced stats, and streak insurance.
